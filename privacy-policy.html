Privacy Policy — WSUS Publisher Last updated: 2026-07-18 WSUS Publisher is a private, single-user application built by Oussama (Wall Street Untold Stories) to automate publishing the channel's own video content to its own TikTok account. It is not a public product and is not used by, or offered to, any third party. What this app does: - Uses TikTok's Content Posting API, authorized via OAuth, to upload and publish videos to the developer's own TikTok account. - Does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data belonging to TikTok users, viewers, or any third party. - Does not access, read, or store any data from any TikTok account other than the single account explicitly authorized by its owner. Data stored: - An OAuth access/refresh token authorizing this app to post to the developer's own TikTok account. This token is stored locally and privately, is never shared with any third party, and can be revoked at any time from the developer's TikTok account settings. - No analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, or cookies are used by this application.
